Rosa Torres

Hello, my name is Rosa Torres. I'm a 17 year old helping to make the internet become a better, supportive, and loving place for self development. I enjoy swimming, reading, being active, taking photos of breath taking locations, music, dancing,and trying new things. My working mission is to help people change their meaning of beauty based on what they see on social media but instead make themselves their own definition. This website helps my mission because many of these amazing locations don't recive much credit but are beautiful. It takes a wise person to truly identify that beauty is within.
