About the Site

Main Focus

Here is what this website will offer: locations that are filled with activities and sights, music suggestions to go along with each location, interesting book reccomendations that go along with the place.

Inspiration

We all love traveling,trying new things yet there is a lot to try- and it's overwhelming. Here we have selected locations that are wonderful to visit and new suggestions for you to try so you don't feel overwhelmed. Not only that we make sure your moments are memorable by these music and book suggestions to make your visit to the location special!

How To

Look over at the Navigation bar and choose a country/ state of your choice. Then look at the various locations found in the area, and then click on each of the individual box to know about the location, music suggestions, book suggestions- and you're set!
